Infrastructure & Facilities

    Lower Ground

  1. Standard size football ground
  2. 8 line 400 metre mud track with lights
  3. Arena for field events like long jump, high jump, shot put, discuss throw, javelin throw, etc
  4. Full length pavilion on one side length-wise.

    Upper Ground

  1. Medium size cricket ground with turf pitch
  2. Practice turf pitches
  3. Practice concrete pitches
  4. Full length pavilion on one side length-wise.

    Sports Complex

    Students Activity Centre(SAC)

  1. Fully equipped gymnasium for boys
  2. Fully equipped gymnasium for girls.
  3. Synthetic floor badminton courts (4)
  4. Yoga centre
  5. Boxing ring
  6. Billiards room
  7. Technical and cultural clubs

  1. International standard 6 line swimming pool
